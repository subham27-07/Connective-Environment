Designing an Interface

Design and develop a physical telepresence installation of wirelessly connected large-scale inflatables placed within the UNCC campus or more specifically Storrs building that will enable visitors in each site to physically experience the presence of visitors in other remote sites, questioning our perception of locality, belongingness, and social identity. While there are many communication options and protocols, we will focus on connecting devices through the internet using WiFi.
For the inspiration I looked into the inflatable designs of jose miguel de prada where the concept of is developing an neighbourhood using large-shape changing inflatables.

Designing a network of Inflatables

With the inspiration of working on involvement and engagement of community I decided to develop a network of Inflatable stacked over each other and a network of four which could respresent community and peoples outside the inflatable could engage themselves with the process of inflataion and deflation simultaneously.
