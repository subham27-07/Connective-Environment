Project 1: Reverse Engineering A Mechanically Actuated Toy

Project 1 was about dismatling toy and take a photo of the disassembled toy and of each part. List and label parts, explain what each part does, and how they function to transfer energy. Also, Create an assembly graph of the toy. Each node is a part (even screws are parts); each link is a structural connection between two parts.
I began the process of disassembling a toy with the intention of understanding its inner mechanisms. The toy which restores energy to move forward.

Bike body parts Names

A. Secondary Wheel - Secondary wheels are those which depends on the movement of primary wheels.

B. Bike Body Primary - Main structure of the vehicle.

C. Bike Body Secondary - Additional parts of the vehicle those which does not necessarily have any function but are there for aesthetics

D. Screws have been used attached vehicle parts.

E. Gear_level 4 - Grear of level 4 depends on the movements of gear level 3

F. Gearbox Primary - Primary gearbox handles the attachments of the all the gears.

G. Primary Wheel - Primary Wheels accounts for the movement of the flywheel bike.

H. Secondary Gearbox

I. Axle - axle holds the gears and the heavy plates to bring down the speed of the vehicle.

J. Heavy Axle Plates - Heavy plates bring down the speed of the vehicle.

K. Springs - Spring helped in the assembly of the axle, while assembling the gearbox back spring helps in the assembly of axle with the gearbox

L. Gear Assembly - This controls the movement of the axle so that axle doesn’t becomes too loose.

M. Gear_level 3 - Gear 3 depends on the movements of gear level 2.

N. Gear_level 2 -Gear 2 depends on the movements of gear level 1.

O. Gear_level 1 -Gear 1 is independent of the movement with rest of the gears but it’s the primary source of producing energy in the vehicle and its movement.

Links diagram of bike body parts

Before the toy can be constructed, the primary motor/gears must be configured. Within the motor are four layers that hold the mechanisms in place. An effective way to assemble the toy is to first assemble the gears in primary gear box and then attach the primary gear-box into the secondary gearbox. After that attach the secondary gearbox to bike body parts and screw them.
